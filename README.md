# mtgstrategy.net

A curated archive of the best Magic: The Gathering strategy articles ever written, ranked by community votes.

🔗 **[mtgstrategy.net](https://www.mtgstrategy.net)**

---

## About

mtgstrategy.net is a living reading list for Magic players who want to get better at the game. The ranking is driven by votes — the community decides which articles matter most.

The archive currently includes 75 hand-picked articles spanning theory, deckbuilding, limited, tournament preparation, mental game, and history & culture. Authors include Patrick Chapin, Paulo Vitor Damo da Rosa, Reid Duke, Michael J. Flores, Zvi Mowshowitz, Kai Budde, Frank Karsten, and many others.
