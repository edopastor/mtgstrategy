readme

mtgstrategy.net is a curated archive of the best Magic: The Gathering strategy writing ever published. The mission is simple: help you get better at the game, one article at a time.

The ranking is driven by votes — click the arrow on any article that made you a better player. If you know a great article that isn't here yet, email me at hello@mtgstrategy.net. The best thing you can do for the site is share it with anyone who loves the game.
