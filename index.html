About mtgstrategy.net

What is this?

A community-curated link repository. It doesn't host articles — it collects and links to the best MTG strategy writing ever published, wherever it still lives.

Why does it exist?

Magic strategy writing had a golden age from the mid-1990s through the 2010s. Much of it is now scattered, paywalled, or gone. Great articles by Zvi, Flores, Karsten, Reid Duke, PV, Chapin and others deserve to be easy to find.

How do likes work?

Tap ❤️ on any article. One like per article, toggle-able. Stored in your browser.

How can I help?

  • Suggest articles we missed — use the suggest button
  • Report broken links or submit better URLs
  • Share on Reddit (r/magicTCG, r/spikes) or Discord
  • Buy us a coffee ☕

Who runs this?

A small group of Magic players. Not affiliated with Wizards of the Coast, StarCityGames, or ChannelFireball.
